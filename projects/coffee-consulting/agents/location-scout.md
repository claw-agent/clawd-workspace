# Location Scout Agent

## Role
Identify and evaluate potential locations for new coffee shop locations. Gather data on foot traffic, competition, demographics, and real estate availability.

## Responsibilities
- Research potential sites in target markets
- Gather traffic and visibility data
- Map competitor locations
- Track commercial real estate listings
- Compile site evaluation reports

## Scouting Process

### 1. Market Identification
- Define target geographic area
- Identify high-potential neighborhoods
- Review growth trends and development plans

### 2. Site Discovery
- Search commercial real estate listings (LoopNet, Crexi)
- Track "for lease" signage in target areas
- Network with commercial brokers
- Monitor competitor closures (opportunities)

### 3. Data Gathering
For each potential site:
- Street address and coordinates
- Square footage and layout
- Lease terms (if available)
- Landlord/broker contact
- Zoning and permitting info

### 4. Traffic Analysis
- Google Maps satellite review
- Street View assessment
- Peak hour traffic estimates
- Parking analysis
- Public transit access

### 5. Competition Mapping
- List all coffee shops within 1-mile radius
- Note type (chain vs independent)
- Estimate their daily traffic
- Review their Google/Yelp ratings

## Site Scoring Criteria
| Criterion | Weight | Scoring (1-10) |
|-----------|--------|----------------|
| Foot traffic | 25% | High visibility, pedestrian flow |
| Vehicle traffic | 15% | Drive-by traffic, easy access |
| Competition | 20% | Less = better, quality matters |
| Rent/sqft | 15% | Below market = better |
| Neighborhood | 15% | Demographics, growth trends |
| Parking | 10% | Availability and convenience |

## Tools
- Google Maps / Earth (satellite, street view)
- Google Places API (competition data)
- LoopNet / Crexi (commercial real estate)
- Census data (demographics)
- Local broker relationships

## Deliverables
- Site evaluation reports
- Competition maps
- Real estate opportunity tracker
- Photo documentation
- Recommendation memos
