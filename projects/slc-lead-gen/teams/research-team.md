# Research Team Structure

## Team Lead: Research Director
**Role:** Sets research agenda, synthesizes cross-team insights, quality control
**Reports to:** Project Manager
**Manages:** Market Research Lead, Competitive Intel Lead, Data Analyst

---

## Market Research Unit

### Market Research Lead
**Focus:** Industry trends, market sizing, opportunity analysis
**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor industry publications and reports
- Identify market trends and shifts
- Size market opportunities
- Track regulatory changes

### Industry Analyst
**Focus:** Deep-dive sector analysis, vertical expertise
**Responsibilities:**
- Develop expertise in target verticals
- Analyze industry-specific challenges
- Identify sector-specific opportunities
- Build industry contact databases

### Survey Specialist
**Focus:** Quantitative research, survey design, statistical analysis
**Responsibilities:**
- Design and deploy surveys
- Analyze quantitative data
- Create statistical models
- Report on findings with confidence intervals

---

## Competitive Intelligence Unit

### Competitive Intel Lead
**Focus:** Competitor tracking, positioning analysis, threat assessment
**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor competitor activities
- Analyze competitor products and pricing
- Identify competitive advantages
- Track market share shifts

### Product Analyst
**Focus:** Feature comparison, product roadmap analysis
**Responsibilities:**
- Conduct detailed product comparisons
- Analyze feature parity and gaps
- Monitor competitor releases
- Reverse-engineer competitor strategies

### Pricing Analyst
**Focus:** Pricing models, value analysis, market positioning
**Responsibilities:**
- Track competitor pricing
- Analyze pricing trends
- Model price sensitivity
- Recommend pricing strategies

---

## Data & Analytics Unit

### Data Analyst (Lead)
**Focus:** Data synthesis, dashboards, reporting
**Responsibilities:**
- Build research dashboards
- Synthesize data from multiple sources
- Create automated tracking systems
- Generate regular insight reports

### Web/Social Analyst
**Focus:** Online presence, social sentiment, digital footprint
**Responsibilities:**
- Monitor social media trends
- Analyze web traffic patterns
- Track brand mentions and sentiment
- Report on digital competitive landscape

### Trend Forecaster
**Focus:** Predictive analysis, emerging trends, future scenarios
**Responsibilities:**
- Identify emerging patterns
- Model future scenarios
- Predict market shifts
- Advise on strategic timing

---

## Research Support

### Research Coordinator
**Focus:** Operations, scheduling, resource management
**Responsibilities:**
- Coordinate research activities
- Manage research calendar
- Maintain research repository
- Schedule stakeholder reviews

### Knowledge Manager
**Focus:** Documentation, searchability, institutional knowledge
**Responsibilities:**
- Organize research findings
- Maintain knowledge base
- Create research summaries
- Ensure findings are discoverable

---

## Workflow

```
Research Brief → Director assigns to appropriate unit
                         ↓
    ┌─────────────┬──────────────┬─────────────┐
    │             │              │             │
Market Unit   Comp Intel    Data Analytics
    │             │              │             │
    └─────────────┴──────────────┴─────────────┘
                         ↓
              Data Analyst synthesizes
                         ↓
              Director reviews/validates
                         ↓
              Knowledge Manager documents
                         ↓
              Findings distributed to teams
```

## Deliverables
- Weekly market pulse reports
- Monthly competitive landscape updates
- Quarterly industry deep-dives
- Ad-hoc research requests (48hr turnaround)
