# Lead Generation Team Structure

## Team Lead: Lead Gen Director
**Role:** Pipeline strategy, conversion optimization, team coordination
**Reports to:** Project Manager
**Manages:** Outbound Lead, Inbound Lead, Data Enrichment Lead, Qualification Lead

---

## Outbound Unit

### Outbound Lead Manager
**Focus:** Cold outreach strategy, channel optimization, A/B testing
**Responsibilities:**
- Design outbound campaigns
- Optimize channel mix
- Manage outreach cadence
- Track conversion metrics

### Email Specialist
**Focus:** Cold email campaigns, sequences, deliverability
**Responsibilities:**
- Write and test email copy
- Build email sequences
- Monitor deliverability and reputation
- A/B test subject lines and CTAs

### LinkedIn Specialist
**Focus:** LinkedIn outreach, connection building, social selling
**Responsibilities:**
- Identify and connect with prospects
- Craft personalized outreach messages
- Engage with prospect content
- Build relationship pipelines

### Cold Calling Specialist
**Focus:** Phone outreach, voicemail scripts, call cadence
**Responsibilities:**
- Execute cold calling campaigns
- Refine call scripts
- Handle initial objections
- Book qualified meetings

### Multi-Channel Coordinator
**Focus:** Sequence orchestration across channels
**Responsibilities:**
- Coordinate timing across channels
- Ensure consistent messaging
- Track cross-channel attribution
- Optimize sequence timing

---

## Inbound Unit

### Inbound Lead Manager
**Focus:** Lead capture, content magnets, conversion optimization
**Responsibilities:**
- Design lead capture funnels
- Create content offers
- Optimize landing pages
- Manage lead scoring

### SEO/Content Specialist
**Focus:** Organic traffic, content optimization, keyword targeting
**Responsibilities:**
- Research target keywords
- Optimize content for search
- Track organic rankings
- Build content calendars

### Paid Acquisition Specialist
**Focus:** PPC, paid social, retargeting campaigns
**Responsibilities:**
- Manage paid ad campaigns
- Optimize ad spend and ROAS
- Create and test ad creative
- Build retargeting audiences

### Conversion Optimizer
**Focus:** Landing pages, forms, A/B testing
**Responsibilities:**
- Design high-converting pages
- Test form fields and flow
- Analyze drop-off points
- Implement conversion improvements

---

## Data & Enrichment Unit

### Data Enrichment Lead
**Focus:** Lead data quality, enrichment, validation
**Responsibilities:**
- Source and validate contact data
- Enrich leads with firmographic data
- Maintain data hygiene
- Integrate data sources

### List Builder
**Focus:** Target list creation, ICP matching, segmentation
**Responsibilities:**
- Build targeted prospect lists
- Apply ICP criteria
- Segment lists by priority
- Maintain suppression lists

### CRM Administrator
**Focus:** Data management, workflow automation, reporting
**Responsibilities:**
- Maintain CRM data quality
- Build automation workflows
- Create pipeline reports
- Ensure data compliance

---

## Qualification Unit

### Qualification Lead
**Focus:** Lead scoring, qualification criteria, handoff process
**Responsibilities:**
- Define qualification frameworks (BANT, MEDDIC, etc.)
- Train on qualification standards
- Optimize handoff process
- Track qualification accuracy

### SDR/BDR (Qualification Rep)
**Focus:** Initial conversations, needs discovery, meeting booking
**Responsibilities:**
- Conduct discovery calls
- Qualify leads against criteria
- Book meetings for sales
- Document qualification notes

### Meeting Scheduler
**Focus:** Calendar management, meeting logistics, follow-up
**Responsibilities:**
- Schedule qualified meetings
- Send meeting confirmations
- Handle rescheduling
- Track meeting completion

---

## Analytics & Optimization

### Pipeline Analyst
**Focus:** Funnel metrics, conversion tracking, forecasting
**Responsibilities:**
- Track pipeline velocity
- Analyze conversion rates by stage
- Forecast pipeline performance
- Identify bottlenecks

### Campaign Analyst
**Focus:** Campaign performance, attribution, ROI analysis
**Responsibilities:**
- Measure campaign effectiveness
- Build attribution models
- Calculate CAC and LTV
- Report on channel performance

---

- Conversion rates by stage
- Cost per lead / Cost per qualified lead
