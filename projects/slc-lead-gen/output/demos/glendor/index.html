Features

Everything you need to One Solution for Every Medical Data Type

Glendor PHI Sanitizer processes your entire data ecosystem—automatically, at-source, without templates or manual intervention.

Fully Automatic Processing

No tuning. No templates. No manual intervention. Glendor automatically identifies and redacts PHI across your medical data, eliminating the labor costs and bottlenecks that make manual de-identification impossible to scale.

At-Source Sanitization

Your medical data is sanitized on your premises or in your cloud—never transmitted to Glendor or any third party. Zero exposure means zero anxiety about where sensitive patient information might end up.

Multi-Modal, Multi-Format

Medical images, pathology slides, clinical reports, videos, photos, voice recordings—one solution handles them all. Stop stitching together point solutions for each data type.

Complete Pixel and Metadata Redaction

PHI hides in more places than pixels. Glendor sanitizes both visual content and embedded metadata in medical images, catching the sensitive information that visual-only solutions miss entirely.

1-Minute Installation

Install and start processing in under a minute. Designed to integrate as a seamless node into your existing data workflows—or operate standalone. Minimal IT overhead, immediate time-to-value.

No BAA Required

Because your data never leaves your premises, no Business Associate Agreement with Glendor is necessary. Simplified procurement, reduced legal overhead, faster deployment.
