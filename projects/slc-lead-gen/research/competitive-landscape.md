# SLC Lead Gen - Competitive Landscape Brief
*Intel Report: January 2026*

---

## Executive Summary

The Salt Lake City web design and lead gen market is **crowded but fragmented**. There are 20+ agencies competing, but most fall into two camps:
1. **Enterprise-focused agencies** (targeting mid-market to Fortune 500)
2. **Generic WordPress shops** (commodity service, race to bottom)

**The gap we can exploit:** Very few agencies focus specifically on **SMB lead generation with measurable ROI guarantees**. Most sell "web design" rather than "customer acquisition."

## Pricing Intelligence

### Industry Benchmarks (from Thumbtack + research)
- **Basic website (5-page):** $500-$2,000
- **Custom WordPress site:** $2,000-$8,000
- **Custom site with e-commerce:** $5,000-$15,000
- **Full branding + website package:** $10,000-$50,000+

### Hourly Rates (SLC market)
- Basic design: $15-$40/hr
- CMS development: $20-$50/hr  
- Custom code: $45-$75/hr
- Strategy/consulting: $100-$200/hr

### Monthly Retainers (observed)
- SEO only: $500-$2,500/mo
- PPC management: $500-$3,000/mo (+ ad spend)
- Full digital marketing: $2,000-$10,000/mo
- Maintenance plans: $50-$500/mo
