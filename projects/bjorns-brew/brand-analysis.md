### Origin Story
Bjorn's Brew was named after **Bjorn**, the beloved Australian Shepherd mascot. The company celebrates his life and legacy through their ongoing mission to support local animal charities. The business was built from the ground up in 2008, changed ownership in 2014, and has since grown to three locations.

## Competitive Positioning

### In the SLC Coffee Market
Bjorn's Brew occupies a unique position:
- **Not corporate** (unlike Starbucks, Dutch Bros)
- **Mission-driven** (charity differentiator)
- **Drive-thru focused** (convenient but not impersonal)
- **Local favorite** with cult following

### Key Competitors (from Yelp/Instagram)
- Sunroom Coffee
- The People's Coffee
- Sunset Coffee
- Other local SLC coffee shops

### Differentiation
While other local shops may have quality coffee, Bjorn's unique combination of:
1. Animal charity mission
2. Drive-thru convenience
3. Pet-friendly culture
4. Cookie-on-lid tradition
5. Strong community connection

...creates a defensible, emotionally resonant brand position.
