# Executive Summary

# Consultant Recommendations

Based on synthesis of both focus groups, here are the prioritized recommendations:

## Immediate Actions (This Week)

1. **Add a Contact Form**
   - Impact: Medium | Effort: Low
   - Users expect it, absence creates friction
   - Include fields: Name, Email, Message, Location preference

2. **Clarify Menu Sizes**
   - Impact: Medium | Effort: Low
   - Add "12oz / 16oz / 20oz" or S/M/L with pricing
   - Reduces pre-visit anxiety

3. **Add Phone Number**
   - Impact: Medium | Effort: Minimal
   - Trust signal, especially for older demographics
   - Click-to-call on mobile

## Short-Term Actions (This Month)

4. **Professional Photo Session**
   - Impact: High | Effort: Medium
   - Capture: locations (interior/exterior), drinks, staff, dogs
   - Transforms the entire site feel

5. **Implement Clear CTAs**
   - Add "Order Now" or "Find a Location" button in hero
   - Sticky header CTA on scroll
   - Each page should guide users forward

6. **Specific Charity Features**
   - Name the three charity partners
   - Include their logos and brief descriptions
   - Add "Meet the Charities" section

## Medium-Term Actions (Next Quarter)

7. **Online Ordering Integration**
   - Partner: Toast, Square, or DoorDash
   - Expected impact: 15-25% revenue increase
   - Addresses #1 customer request

8. **Instagram Integration**
   - Embed #bjornsbrew feed on homepage
   - Creates dynamic social proof
   - Encourages user-generated content

9. **Location Photos & Maps**
   - Add photo gallery per location
   - Embed Google Maps
   - Include parking information

## Long-Term Considerations (6+ Months)

10. **Loyalty Program**
    - "Paw Points" or similar branding
    - Drives repeat visits
    - Valuable customer data

11. **Content/Blog Section**
    - Monthly charity spotlights
    - Coffee sourcing stories
    - Community features

# Conclusion

Bjorn's Brew has built a website with a **strong emotional foundation** and **clear brand identity**. The mission-driven narrative resonates deeply with both casual consumers and design professionals.

The primary gaps are **functional rather than brand-related**:
- No online ordering (biggest miss)
- Limited photography (relies too heavily on text/icons)
- Missing contact form
- Menu lacks detail (sizes, photos)

With the recommended improvements, Bjorn's Brew can transform from a **good informational site** into a **high-converting customer acquisition and retention tool**.

**Estimated investment:** $5K-15K for professional photography + ordering integration
**Estimated ROI:** 20-30% increase in new customer visits, 15-25% increase in revenue through mobile ordering
