## üìä COMMON DESIGN PATTERNS

### Color Palettes
| Pattern | Examples | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------|
| **Warm Neutrals** | Cream, tan, warm white | Most common ‚Äî evokes coffee, comfort |
| **Black & White** | High contrast | Premium/sophisticated positioning |
| **Earth Tones** | Browns, sage greens | Sustainability focus |
| **Accent Colors** | Gold, burnt orange, deep burgundy | Used sparingly for CTAs |

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, clean sans-serif (often custom or geometric)
- **Body:** Readable serif or clean sans-serif
- **Accent:** Sometimes handwritten or script for warmth
- **Trend:** Large, confident typography for hero statements

### Hero Section Patterns
1. **Product Hero** ‚Äî Coffee bag front and center (Verve, Stumptown)
2. **Lifestyle Hero** ‚Äî People enjoying coffee/cafe scenes (Onyx)
3. **Message Hero** ‚Äî Bold typography statement (Intelligentsia)
4. **Video Hero** ‚Äî Dynamic cafe or brewing footage (Trade)

### Navigation Structure
Most sites use:
- **Shop** ‚Üí Coffee, Equipment, Merch, Gifts
- **Subscribe** ‚Üí Prominent placement
- **About** ‚Üí Story, Team, Sourcing
- **Locations** ‚Üí Cafe finder
- **Learn/Brew** ‚Üí Educational content

### Product Display Best Practices
- **Tasting Notes** visible on cards
- **Roast Level** clearly indicated
- **Origin/Region** displayed
- **Price** visible without click
- **Quick Add** buttons for speed
- **Badges** (New, Bestseller, Limited, Sold Out)

### Social Proof Patterns
1. **Star Ratings** ‚Äî 4.9/5 stars prominent
2. **Review Count** ‚Äî "100,000+ customers"
3. **Verified Buyer Tags** ‚Äî Builds trust
4. **Location Attribution** ‚Äî "Michael I., Santa Cruz, CA"
5. **Awards/Press** ‚Äî Logos and competition wins

### Subscription UX
- **Quiz/Personalization** ‚Äî Most sites offer this
- **Flexible Scheduling** ‚Äî "Set your schedule"
- **Free Shipping** ‚Äî On subscriptions
- **Easy Cancel** ‚Äî Reduce friction
- **Savings Messaging** ‚Äî "Save 10%"

## üéØ RECOMMENDED PATTERNS FOR BJORN'S BREW

### Must-Have Elements

#### 1. **Strong Hero Section**
- Bold headline that captures Bjorn's personality/story
- High-quality lifestyle or product imagery
- Clear CTA to shop or subscribe

#### 2. **Coffee Quiz**
- "Find Your Perfect Brew" personalization
- 4-6 questions about taste preferences
- Leads to product recommendation

#### 3. **Product Cards with Tasting Notes**
```
[Image]
Ethiopian Yirgacheffe
BRIGHT & FRUITY ¬∑ LIGHT ROAST
Notes: Blueberry ‚Ä¢ Jasmine ‚Ä¢ Citrus
$18.00
[Add to Cart]
```

#### 4. **Subscription Section**
Three pillars:
1. Fresh roasted weekly
2. Free shipping always
3. Skip or cancel anytime

#### 5. **The Bjorn's Story**
- Origin story (when, why, who)
- What makes Bjorn's different
- Sourcing philosophy
- Local community connection

#### 6. **Location/Visit Us**
- Beautiful cafe photos
- Address, hours, map
- "Come say hi" casual tone

#### 7. **Social Proof**
- Customer reviews with photos
- "Join X happy customers"
- Any press mentions or awards

### Nice-to-Have Elements

- **Now Playing** music ticker (unique like Onyx)
- **Brew Guides** educational content
- **Producer Stories** for origin transparency
- **Limited/Seasonal Releases** for urgency
- **Equipment section** if selling gear
- **Blog/Learn section** for SEO

### Color Recommendations for Bjorn's Brew
Based on "Bjorn" (Scandinavian name):
- **Primary:** Warm cream/off-white (#FAF7F2)
- **Secondary:** Deep forest green (#2D4A3E) or midnight blue (#1E3A4C)
- **Accent:** Burnt orange (#CC6B3D) or gold (#C9A962)
- **Text:** Rich dark brown (#3D2B1F) or charcoal (#333333)

This creates a **Nordic-inspired warmth** ‚Äî cozy yet sophisticated.

### Typography Recommendations
- **Headlines:** Something bold with character (Playfair Display, Fraunces, or custom)
- **Body:** Clean and readable (Inter, Source Sans, or similar)
- **Accent:** A touch of warmth for quotes or special sections

### Mobile Experience Priorities
1. **Fast loading** ‚Äî Compress images, lazy load
2. **Sticky Cart Button** ‚Äî Easy checkout access
3. **Swipeable Product Carousels** ‚Äî Natural mobile interaction
4. **Collapsible Menus** ‚Äî Keep it clean
5. **Touch-Friendly CTAs** ‚Äî 44px minimum tap targets
6. **SMS/Mobile Subscription Option** ‚Äî Text updates
