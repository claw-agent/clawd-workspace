# GOLFn Second App: Concept Exploration
## AI Focus Group Analysis

**Date:** January 28, 2026
**Objective:** Identify the optimal second app concept for GOLFn brand extension

---

## Executive Summary

Through AI focus group methodology with 15 diverse personas, we evaluated 7 app concepts that could extend the GOLFn brand beyond golf while maintaining its core DNA: "get paid for what you love" + premium brands + collector psychology + community through activity.

**Top Recommendation: TRAILn (Outdoor Adventure Rewards)**
- Highest overall score: 8.4/10
- Strongest brand fit with GOLFn's premium positioning
- Massive untapped market ($887B outdoor recreation)
- Natural demographic overlap with GOLFn users

**Runner-Up: RACQn (Tennis/Pickleball Rewards)**
- Score: 8.1/10
- Clearest activity parallel to golf
- Premium brand partnerships readily available

**Honorable Mention: RIDEn (Cycling Rewards)**
- Score: 7.8/10
- Strong existing gear culture
- Proven app market (Strava)
