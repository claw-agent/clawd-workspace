## DARROW (Protagonist)

### Pre-Carving (Red) [Book 1]
- **Hair:** Red/rust-red. "rustyheaded lads" (Fitchner)
- **Eyes:** Red — "blood-red eyes" is the Red trait
- **Skin:** Pale, scarred. "I am pale and wiry, young and scarred"
- **Build:** Young, muscular for a Red but small by Gold standards. "Once, I thought myself a handsome man of the clans. Strong. Muscular. Up here, I am pale and wiry, young and scarred." "and still growing tall"
- **Scars:** Steam scars on chest: "Eo rolls to look at me and traces the steam scars that run like rivers down my chest. Farther down she'd find scars from the pitviper along my belly." Burns on hands: "My hands are scarred, burned things."
- **Hands:** Legendary Helldiver grip. "Nothing is stronger than a Helldiver's grip."

### Post-Carving (Gold) [Book 1]
- **Hair:** Golden. "When Evey is done cutting the golden hair, she stands back and stares at me."
- **Eyes:** Gold. "My eyes shine like ingots." "golden eyes"
- **Skin:** "My skin is soft and rich." "tanned, and dexterous." "My skin is too soft, too lustrous, too faultless. I don't know my body without scars."
- **Build:** Tall, lean, powerfully muscled. "My shoulders grow broader, rounded; I see tendons emerge in my forearms; a tense mass of hard muscles bind my torso, like armor." "I thicken."
- **Hands:** "My hands are freaks. They are smooth, tanned, and dexterous, as any Gold's should be. But there is a power in them out of proportion with the rest of me. If I am a blade, they are my edge." "With a simple squeeze, I can pulverize rock."
- **Sigils:** Golden wings on hands. "The circle is set in the center of each hand's backside. Wings swoop back along the flesh, curving like scythes up the sides of my wristbones."
- **Distinguishing features:** Red Helldiver sweatband. Carries a slingBlade.
- **Mannerisms:** Hums while working with his hands. Fingers his wedding band thinking of Eo.
- **Overall:** "I am Gold. And I am as cold as it." "You're beautiful" — Evey. Mickey calls him "like a stallion, one of the old stallions of Earth."

### After Imprisonment [Book 3]
- **Condition after the box:** "I am a corpse. Skeletal and pallid. Ribs erupt from skin thinner than the film atop heated milk. Knees jut from spindly legs. Toenails have grown long and grasping. Scars from the Jackal's torture mottle my flesh. Muscle has withered. And tubes that kept me alive in the darkness erupt from my belly, black and stringy umbilical cords still anchoring me to the floor of my cell." [Book 3]
- **Beard:** "my ratty beard" [Book 3]
- **Hair shaved:** "Dirty blond hair puddles onto the concrete as the Gray finishes scalping me with an electric razor." [Book 3]
- **Overall appearance post-rescue:** "a tottering, withered form, dragging my friends, sunken eyes, face like that of a starving old demon, bearded and ridiculous" [Book 3]
- **Withered state:** "I'm just a withered husk of a man. I run my fingers against my cage of ribs. So many slender little things." [Book 3]
- **Darrow as "withered fossil":** "I can only curl in a ball, a withered fossil of the man that was. Hands cuffed behind my back. Naked on cold rock." [Book 3]
- **Sigils removed:** "my hands bear no Sigils. Removed by Mickey when I was Carved in Tinos. I am the first soul in hundreds of years to walk without them." [Book 3]
- **Right hand lost:** Cut off by the Jackal. "my missing right hand" / "the stump of my right hand" / "where my right hand used to be" [Book 3]
- **Hand reattached:** "Mickey and Virany reattached my hand, which I retrieved from the Jackal's shuttle that set down on Luna. It will be months before I can write again, much less use a blade." [Book 3]
- **Razor:** "It is my razor. The one given to me by Mustang...It is white and smooth as a child's first tooth." Now etched with images: "Eo looks back at me. An image of her etched into the metal...crouched, hair messy about her shoulders, picking a haemanthus from the ground, looking up, just about to smile. And above Eo is my father kissing my mother at the door of our home." [Book 3]

## SEVRO

### Book 1
- **Build:** Very small. "squat ugly little Goblin." "smallest boy in the Institute"
- **Eyes:** "Circles ring his reckless eyes." Dark, penetrating.
- **Appearance:** Ugly by Gold standards. Mud-covered, feral.
- **Wolf pelt:** "He's wearing the pelt of the wolf he killed. It covers his head to his shins."
- **Mannerisms:** Insulting everything, feral behavior, eating raw food. Carries knives.
- **Nicknames:** Goblin, Little Goblin. Followers: Howlers.

### Book 3
- **Hair:** Mohawk. "His hair a dirty, sweat-soaked Mohawk." [Book 3]
- **Face:** "hatchet face split with a jagged grin" [Book 3]
- **Eyes:** Now has Darrow's original red eyes implanted by Mickey. "Your eyes…" — Darrow, noticing. "Mickey had your eyes in a cryobox at his joint in Yorkton...I figured you weren't usin' 'em, so...I asked if he'd put 'em in. You know. Bring us closer together. Something to remember you by." [Book 3]
- **Tattoos:** Wolf tattoos and skull tattoos. "Wolf tattoos looking evil against his pale skin in the industrial light." / Skull tattoo given by Ragnar — "He holds up his right hand to show me his skull tattoo." [Book 3]
- **Skin:** Pale. "pale skin" [Book 3]
- **Contacts:** Wears black contacts over the red eyes for missions. "eyes smoldering behind his black contacts" [Book 3]
- **Armor/helm:** Wears his father Fitchner's spiked Ares helmet — "a crimson, armored man with the spiked helmet of his father." DemonHelm for stealth missions — "pupil black and shaped subtly like a screaming demon face." [Book 3]
- **Size relative to Obsidians:** "He's tiny for an Obsidian male, but still scarred and tattooed and terrifying" [Book 3]
- **As Ares:** Wears the crimson armor and spiked sunburst helmet. Red eyes glowing from the helmet. [Book 3]
- **Mannerisms:** Eating candy bars, popping gum (inherited from Fitchner), reckless energy, cackling laughter, howling. [Book 3]
