## DARROW (Protagonist)

### Pre-Carving (Red)
- **Hair:** Red/rust-red. Not explicitly described in detail on Darrow himself, but implied same as all Reds — "rustyheaded lads" (Fitchner, ~line 3826)
- **Eyes:** Red — "blood-red eyes" is the Red trait (Dancer's described at ~line 1640)
- **Skin:** Pale, scarred. "I am pale and wiry, young and scarred" (~line 2233)
- **Build:** Young, muscular for a Red but small by Gold standards. "Once, I thought myself a handsome man of the clans. Strong. Muscular. Up here, I am pale and wiry, young and scarred." (~line 2232). "and still growing tall" (~line 264)
- **Scars:** Steam scars on chest: "Eo rolls to look at me and traces the steam scars that run like rivers down my chest. Farther down she'd find scars from the pitviper along my belly." (~line 896-897). Burns on hands from helium-3 mining. "My hands are scarred, burned things. When Eo kissed them, they grew gentle for love." (~line 1957)
- **Hands:** Legendary Helldiver grip. "Nothing is stronger than a Helldiver's grip." (~line 2692)
- **Key quote:** "their golden son" — what his people call him (~line 795)

### Post-Carving (Gold)
- **Hair:** Golden. "When Evey is done cutting the golden hair, she stands back and stares at me." (~line 2605)
- **Eyes:** Gold. "My eyes shine like ingots." (~line 2603). "golden eyes" (~line 2693). "When they take my eyes and give me ones of gold, I feel dead inside." (~line 2458)
- **Skin:** "My skin is soft and rich." (~line 2603). "tanned, and dexterous" (~line 2575). "My skin is too soft, too lustrous, too faultless. I don't know my body without scars." (~line 2464-2465)
- **Build:** Tall, lean, powerfully muscled. "My shoulders grow broader, rounded; I see tendons emerge in my forearms; a tense mass of hard muscles bind my torso, like armor." (~line 2564). "I thicken." (~line 2562). Tall as Cassius and Priam — both described as very tall Golds.
- **Hands:** "My hands are freaks. They are smooth, tanned, and dexterous, as any Gold's should be. But there is a power in them out of proportion with the rest of me. If I am a blade, they are my edge." (~line 2574-2575). "With a simple squeeze, I can pulverize rock." (~line 2567)
- **Sigils:** Golden wings on hands. "The circle is set in the center of each hand's backside. Wings swoop back along the flesh, curving like scythes up the sides of my wristbones." (~line 2610-2611)
- **Distinguishing features:** Wears a red Helldiver sweatband — his talisman throughout. "I take my red sweatband off of my head and dab away the sweat on my face, then wrap it around my head once more." (~line 10349). Carries a slingBlade throughout the Institute.
- **Mannerisms:** Hums while working with his hands. Fingers his wedding band when thinking of Eo.
- **Overall:** "I am Gold. And I am as cold as it." (~line 2602). "You're beautiful" — Evey (~line 2607). Mickey calls him "like a stallion, one of the old stallions of Earth" (~line 2538).

## EO

- **Hair:** Red/rust-red, described as a mane. "Little Eo—a tiny girl hidden beneath a mane of red. Red like the rock around me, not true red, rust-red. Red like our home, like Mars." (~line 171). "her rusty hair swirled" (~line 459). "a mane of red" (~line 1223). Her hair shifts in ventilation: "Her face is pale as chalk and her hair shifts slightly as the ventilators rasp above." (~line 1399)
- **Eyes:** Red (standard Red trait). "Her eyes find mine. They shine like rubies" (~line 1186). "Eo has eyes only for me" (~line 1262)
- **Skin:** Pale. "pale skin" (~line 1071). "Her skin prickles" (~line 1181)
- **Build:** Tiny, thin, delicate. "a tiny girl" (~line 171). "she could be made from air, from the ether that binds the stars" (~line 173). "I put my arm around her shoulders. They are thinner than they used to be" (~line 635). "Eo lies and says she's never got much of an appetite" (~line 264). "She's a fragile thing" — said of her sister Dio, but Eo is similarly described.
- **Age:** Sixteen (~line 172)
- **Clothing:** Skirts — "Eo tucks the bread into her skirts for later" (~line 621). Ribbons in hair at festivals — "can put ridiculous ribbons in their hair" (~line 677)
- **Physical mannerisms:** Dancing — her defining trait. "her feet moved with the zither and her hips to the drums" (~line 459-460). "Hers was a graceful, proud movement." (~line 462). Jaw flexing when angry: "Her jaw flexes" (~line 919) and "The muscle in her jaw flexes" (~line 766)
- **Wedding band:** Woven from her own hair: "This is the girl who wove me a wedding band of her own hair." (~line 1224)
- **Key appearance quote:** "But never has Eo been more beautiful to me than in that moment. In the face of cold power, she is fire. This is the girl who danced through the smoky tav with a mane of red." (~line 1222-1223)
- **At death:** "She hangs like a doll. Her face is pale as chalk" (~line 1399). "Even now she is beautiful, even drained of the light" (~line 1176)

## MUSTANG / VIRGINIA AU AUGUSTUS

- **Hair:** Golden, in braids, long. "Hair so golden it sparkles in the sun and flows down her back in braids." (~line 5522-5523). "I'm left with a handful of coiled gold" — when Darrow grabs her braids (~line 5558). "Her golden hair is wild." (~line 10393)
- **Eyes:** Golden with flakes. "Golden flakes spiral there in the irises." (~line 10430)
- **Face:** Heart-shaped, quick. "She's all laughing eyes and a smirking mouth in a face shaped like a heart." (~line 5521-5522). "Her quick face" (~line 5824)
- **Build:** Small, delicate body but fierce presence. "She is small, delicate. Her smile is not. It mocks us." (~line 5488-5489)
- **Distinguishing features:** Rides a dappled mustang horse. Streaks her face with war paint: "Her face is streaked with three diagonal lines of blue berry juice mixed with animal fat." (~line 5479-5480). "bloody from the skirmish" (~line 5920-5921)
- **Clothing/armor:** Black tunic with knife underneath (~line 5543). Later: dirty cloak — "Her cloak dirtier than my own. It hangs brown and gray, not white. She smells like smoke and hunger." (~line 10394-10395)
- **Mannerisms:** Smirking, playful intelligence. "Her eyes sparkle like a fox's might." (~line 5529). Suppresses laughs. Leans forward on saddle pommel: "She leans forward, arms crossed over the pommel of her saddle." (~line 5819)
- **Cassius's assessment:** "she is something to look at" (~line 5530). "Mustang is a s-sexp-p-pot" (~line 5618)
- **Draft:** Chosen higher than 10th pick.
- **Key quote:** "Well, aren't you the charmer, handsome." (~line 5499)

## SEVRO

- **Build:** Very small, the smallest in the Institute. "squat ugly little Goblin" (~line 4270). "smallest boy in the Institute" (~line 4524). "Damn small kid" (~line 5655)
- **Eyes:** "Circles ring his reckless eyes." (~line 4337). Dark, penetrating: "his gaze penetrates too deep" (~line 6561)
- **Appearance:** Ugly by Gold standards. "squat ugly little Goblin" (~line 4270). Mud-covered, feral. "The gold of his black fatigues is coated in mud. So's his face." (~line 5656)
- **Wolf pelt:** His signature — wears the skin of a wolf he killed. "He's wearing the pelt of the wolf he killed. It covers his head to his shins." (~line 5654-5655). "running along the hillsides at night in his wolfskin, looking, as Cassius put it best, 'like some sort of hairy demonchild on hallucinogens.'" (~line 4995-4996)
- **Mannerisms:** Insulting everything, feral behavior, eating raw food. "Sevro walks around all normalish—insulting everything that moves" (~line 4998). Dragging dead wolves by their legs (~line 4566-4567). Carries knives. "Sevro pulls out his knives" (~line 9906)
- **Key quote from Cassius:** "Oh, y-you are b-beautiful, Goblin. B-beautiful, beautiful b-b-oy. And smelly." (~line 5658)
- **Nicknames:** Goblin, Little Goblin. His followers: Howlers (originally "Toadstools" by enemies)

## CASSIUS AU BELLONA

- **Hair:** Golden curls. "He runs a hand back through his curls." (~line 3634). "golden head" (~line 4871). Later in siege: "His hair is golden curls but matted with grit and grease. Fleas make their home there." (~line 10687)
- **Eyes:** Lively. "His lively eyes dance over my face" (~line 3039-3040). "the spark in his eye has cooled" (~line 10689)
- **Build:** Tall, broad-shouldered. "He's tall as me, but broad in the shoulders." (~line 3637-3638, said of Priam but Cassius is similarly tall). "broad smile" (~line 3634)
- **Face:** Extremely handsome. "I don't think I've ever seen a more perfect human being. A bloodydamn statue." (~line 3638-3639, said of Priam; Cassius similarly). "He is still beautiful, still more handsome than I ever will be." (~line 10688)
- **Battle damage:** "Cassius's left eye is swollen shut. Lips are split. Ribs purple as grapes. His right hand is broken and the joint of his shoulder is odd." (~line 5420-5422)
- **Mannerisms:** Lounging, charm, bravado. Runs hands through curls. Blows kisses. Laughs frequently. "tied back his pretty hair, swished his razor" (~line 4693)
- **Key quote:** "You are stunningly beautiful. You must be from Venus." — to Mustang (~line 5493-5494)

He is laughing" (~line 9983-9984)
- **Key quote:** "This is a monster in the flesh of a man." — Darrow (~line 9985)
