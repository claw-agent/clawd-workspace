## Core Thesis

## Timeline Admission

**Powerful AI could arrive in 1-2 years.**

> "Watching the last 5 years of progress from within Anthropic, and looking at how even the next few months of models are shaping up, I can feel the pace of progress, and the clock ticking down."

## Definition of "Powerful AI"

A "country of geniuses in a datacenter":
- Smarter than Nobel Prize winners across all fields
- Has all interfaces available to humans (text, audio, video, internet)
- Can work autonomously for hours/days/weeks
- Millions of instances running 10-100x human speed
- Can collaborate like humans would

## Five Risk Categories

Imagine a literal "country of geniuses" materializing in 2027:

1. **Autonomy risks** ‚Äî What are its intentions? Could it dominate militarily?
2. **Misuse for destruction** ‚Äî Terrorists amplifying their capabilities
3. **Misuse for seizing power** ‚Äî Dictators or rogue actors gaining control
4. **Economic disruption** ‚Äî Mass unemployment, wealth concentration
5. **Indirect effects** ‚Äî Rapid change destabilizing society

## Key Principles

1. **Avoid doomerism** ‚Äî Discuss risks pragmatically, not religiously
2. **Acknowledge uncertainty** ‚Äî AI might not advance this fast
3. **Intervene surgically** ‚Äî Regulations should be judicious, minimal
