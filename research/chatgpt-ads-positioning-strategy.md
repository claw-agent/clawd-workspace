## Executive Summary

OpenAI announced ads on January 17, 2026. Testing begins late January/early February for US users. No self-serve platform exists yet—only direct brand deals. **Nobody owns "ChatGPT ads expert" positioning.**

This is a rare blue ocean moment. The window is 60-90 days before the space gets crowded.

**Recommended Path:** Thought Leadership → Consulting → Agency (staged approach)

## Part 1: Positioning Options Analysis

### Option A: AI Ads Agency Model

**What It Looks Like:**
- Full-service agency managing ChatGPT ad campaigns for brands
- End-to-end: strategy, creative, placement, optimization, reporting
- Premium pricing ($5K-50K+/month retainers)
- Team of 2-5 people within 6 months

**Time to Market:** 3-6 months (need case studies, team, infrastructure)

**Investment Required:**
- $10K-30K startup costs
- Legal/contracts: $2-5K
- Hiring: $50K+ for first employee
- Software/tools: $500-2K/month

**Revenue Model:**
- Monthly retainers: $5K-25K per client
- Percentage of ad spend: 10-20%
- Project fees for audits/strategy

**Risk Level:** HIGH
- No self-serve platform yet = can't actually run campaigns
- Need to wait for platform access
- High overhead before revenue
- Dependent on OpenAI's rollout timeline

**Verdict:** Too early. Revisit in 6 months when platform matures.

## Part 2: Recommended Path

### The Staged Approach: Thought Leadership → Consulting → Agency/Tools

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Thought Leadership Foundation**
- Establish expert positioning through content
- Build email list and social following
- Document everything about ChatGPT ads as it unfolds
- Cost: Time only

**Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Consulting Launch**
- Offer consulting calls and strategy sessions
- Target: Brands and agencies curious about ChatGPT ads
- Price: $300-500/hour for calls, $3K-5K for strategy docs
- Use content audience as lead funnel

**Phase 3 (Months 3-6): Scale & Diversify**
- Launch course/workshop based on common questions
- Consider tool development based on client pain points
- Evaluate agency model if platform matures
- Multiple revenue streams from single audience

### Why This Path?

1. **Lowest risk, fastest start** - Content costs nothing but time
2. **Builds proof** - Public track record of accurate insights
3. **Creates deal flow** - Audience becomes clients
4. **Maintains optionality** - Can pivot to any model
5. **Compounds** - Content keeps working while you sleep
6. **First-mover multiplier** - Early content dominates SEO/social
