## The Opportunity

OpenAI announced they're testing ads in ChatGPT (free + Go tiers). This is a **massive new advertising surface** that nobody has cracked yet.

### OpenAI's Stated Principles
- Responses won't be influenced by ads
- Ads are separate and clearly labeled
- Conversations are private from advertisers
- Plus, Pro, Business, Enterprise = no ads

### Why This Matters
1. **First-mover advantage** - Nobody knows how to advertise on AI yet
2. **New ad format** - Not like Google/Meta; conversational context is different
3. **Intent signals** - People ask ChatGPT questions = high intent
4. **Lower competition** - Early days = cheap CPM/CPC
