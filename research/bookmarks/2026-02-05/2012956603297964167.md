   - Maintenance: Repetitive work to keep things running

3. **The Flow Formula:** Challenge must be *just above* your skill level. Too high = anxiety. Too low = boredom.

4. **Successful Creatives Pattern:** They work *less* physically but think constantly. They take rest seriously because the Default Mode Network generates their best ideas.

5. **Daily Structure:**
