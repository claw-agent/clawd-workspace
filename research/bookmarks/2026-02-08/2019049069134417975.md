## The Tweet
> The Agent Will Eat Your System of Record

## Summary
Thesis: AI agents will collapse the "system of record" moat that enterprise software (Salesforce, Workday, NetSuite) has relied on for decades. The data in these systems is surprisingly small (megabytes to gigabytes). Once agents have API access, they cache everything locally, making the original system irrelevant. "We store your data" is no longer a moat.
